Who We Are

Gracious Tent's & Sounds (G.T.S) is your trusted partner in events, celebrations, and funeral services. With years of experience, we specialize in tent hiring and selling, professional sound systems, elegant decorations, and comprehensive funeral solutions.

Our goal is to bring comfort, professionalism, and excellence to every occasion we serve.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional event and funeral services with professionalism, reliability, and care.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of tents, sounds, and funeral services across the region.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Reliability, and Customer Satisfaction.

Our Journey

2010 - Founded

Started with a few tents and small PA systems for family events.

2015 - Expansion

Expanded to full PA systems, corporate and church events.

2018 - Funeral Services

Introduced dignified funeral coordination, hearses, and caskets.

2023 - Regional Leader

Became one of the most trusted names in tents, sounds, and funeral services across Kenya.
