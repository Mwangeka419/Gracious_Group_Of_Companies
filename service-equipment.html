Sound Systems & Event Equipment

Your best partner in PA & Sound — we sell & hire professional sound systems and equipment for corporate, family, church, political, institutional events and more.

Who We Serve

We supply and support audio systems for a wide range of events: corporate meetings, product launches, church services, political rallies, school functions, concerts, weddings and private parties. Our approach is to match equipment and technical support to the scale and goals of your event.

What We Sell & Hire

  • Microphones — wired & wireless handheld, lavalier mics, headset mics.
  • Speakers — mid-range powered speakers, subwoofers and full PA stacks.
  • Mixers & Amps — analog and digital mixers, amplifiers for FOH and monitor setups.
  • Cables & Accessories — XLR, TRS, speaker cables, stands, DI boxes, connectors.
  • DJs & Sound Engineers — pro operators available on request.

Why Choose G.T.S for Sound

  • ✅ Reliable, well-maintained equipment
  • ✅ Flexible hire and purchase options
  • ✅ On-site setup & technical support
  • ✅ Competitive pricing & timely delivery
